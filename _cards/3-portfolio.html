CapiMar

CapiMar is a nautical training college in Dénia, Spain. The project involved updating their website to have a modern appearance and clearer information about their courses and rental boats. As they are part of the same organisation as Formación Capicua, I elected to use a similar basic style using the same premium theme, but customising it in a different way to meet the requirements for the CapiMar brand.
